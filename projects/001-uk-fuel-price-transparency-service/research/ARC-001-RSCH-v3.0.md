## Purpose and Scope

This document captures research findings on Alibaba Cloud services evaluated against the requirements of the UK Fuel Price Transparency Service (ARC-001-REQ-v2.0). It provides a structured assessment of service capabilities, UK regional availability, compliance posture, and suitability for UK Government OFFICIAL-classified workloads under the CMA Fuel Finder programme.

**Scope**: All functional requirements (FR-001 through FR-015), non-functional requirements (NFR-P, NFR-A, NFR-SEC, NFR-C, NFR-I, NFR-M), and integration requirements (INT-001 through INT-008) from ARC-001-REQ-v2.0. The assessment focuses on Alibaba Cloud's ability to host a UK Government digital service that ingests real-time fuel pricing data from ~8,500 forecourts and serves citizens, retailers, enforcement officers, and third-party developers.
